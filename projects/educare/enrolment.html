enrolment

Registrations are done in March for the coming school year. To register, parents or guardians can contact the school for an application or download the application pdf. Click here to download.

When parents enrol their children, a copy of the child's updated Road to Health card must be handed in and a Certified copy of a birth certificate is required.

Parents/Guardians are required to provide a proof of income.

cost

  • Entry fee: R200
  • Fees between R150 and R550 per month depending on the parent's income. (Note that proof of income will be requested on application.)
  • After-care cost: R150 per month additional to above fees.
