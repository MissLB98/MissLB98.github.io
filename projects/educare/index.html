Theresa Jacobs, the Principal

Working at Red Cross Children’s Hospital for twenty seven years in the Occupational Therapy as an assistant inspired me to improve quality of life in children.

It was not just merely a job but a passion that grew stronger, making a difference in young lives by treating children with normal developmental delays and psycho-social development.

The ongoing passion for children inspired me to invest in reaching out to children to experience and gain from my knowledge and skill I have obtained over the years.

Daily Programmes

The following daily programmes will be provided to enrich and build each child’s character and abilities holistically, thriving through learning. "It is a home away from home to children to make them feel safe in a happy and relaxing environment." - Theresa Jacobs

  • Play time with Blocks, Puzzles, Toys, etc.

  • Structured Activity

  • Educational Programmes

  • Music and Movement

  • Story Reading

  • Resting/Nap Time

  • Outdoor Play

  • Healthy food will be provided at meal breaks

Parents should feel free to liaise with the school regarding any concerns or matters at hand and are encouraged to participate in the utilization of the successfulness of the school.

Location

  • The pre-school is near a Taxi and Bus rank/terminal. Within walking distance to parents/guardians living in Grassy Park, Cape Town.

  • Accessible and Commuter Friendly

  • Situated on the Main Road and near other social services such as: local library, doctors, businesses and police station.

  • Please note that we do not provide transport. Children need to be dropped off and picked up by parents/carers. Please see the contact page for the map.
